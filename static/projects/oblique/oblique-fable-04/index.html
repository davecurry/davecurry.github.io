Oblique Strategies

The deck’s whole identity is austerity — a white card, a black box, one sentence. So the design work lives in the interaction: each option below leads with a different metaphor for the same act of drawing a card. Every frame is live — click around.

Assumptions: strict warm monochrome; Helvetica, the deck’s own voice (Option D tries a quiet serif reinterpretation); 32 well-known strategies as working content; motion kept slow and deliberate.

OPTION A

The Draw

The faithful one. The deck sits on a dark table; click it and the top card lifts, flips toward you, and holds. Returning it readies the next. Closest to the physical ritual.

The Spread

The browsable one. The deck is tipped out onto the table, face down. Hover to feel a card lift; click to turn it over in place. Only one card may be face up at a time — turning a new one returns the last.
